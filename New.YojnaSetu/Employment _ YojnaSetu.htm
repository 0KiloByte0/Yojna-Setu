Skills & Employment

Government schemes in India aim to enhance skills and employment by providing training, financial assistance, and support for entrepreneurship and self-employment.  

Himachal Pradesh Labour and Employment Scheme

The Employment Exchange Management Information System (EEMIS) is the official online portal of the Labour & Employment Department, Government of Himachal Pradesh. It facilitates employment services for job seekers, employers, and employment exchange officials.

Learn more

Delhi Employment Exchange Scheme

The Online Employment Portal of De​lhi is an initiative by the Directorate of Employment, Government of the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi. It aims to connect job seekers with potential employers by providing a platform for registration, job postings, and candidate searches.

Learn more

Gov. of West Bengal Initiative

The Employment Bank West Bengal is an initiative by the Labour Department of the Government of West Bengal, designed to connect job seekers with potential employers. The specific page at accept. serves as the Terms and Conditions agreement that job seekers must accept before proceeding with registration on the portal.

Learn more

Bihar Skill Development Mission Portal

The Bihar Skill Development Mission (BSDM) is an initiative by the Government of Bihar aimed at empowering the state's youth by providing them with essential skills to enhance their employability and contribute to Bihar's economic growth.

Learn more

Employment Guarantee Scheme

​The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) portal is the official website of the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India. 

It serves as a comprehensive platform for information, monitoring, and implementation of the MGNREGA scheme, which aims to enhance livelihood security in rural areas by providing at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year.
