Ayushman Bharat Yojana (PM-JAY) Overview

Launched: 23rd September 2018

Launched by: Government of India

Ministry: Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

Full Name: Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

üéØ Objective

To provide health coverage to economically vulnerable Indians and reduce the financial burden of medical treatment.

  • Cashless & Paperless: Treatment is cashless and paperless at the point of service.
  • Portability: Beneficiaries can avail treatment anywhere in India under empanelled hospitals.
  • Empanelled Hospitals: Both public and private hospitals are part of the scheme.

  • Cancer treatment, maternity care, etc.
